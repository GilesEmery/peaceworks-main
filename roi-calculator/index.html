Why This Matters

Culture costs more than most companies realize.

Relational drag shows up as hesitation, unspoken tension, duplicated effort, low trust, defensive leadership, and meetings that never surface the real issue. The cost is usually hidden, but it is not small.

Execution slows

Teams spend energy navigating friction instead of moving forward with clarity.

Trust erodes

People begin protecting themselves, which quietly changes the emotional atmosphere of the whole company.

Leaders carry too much

Senior leaders absorb unresolved tension they were never trained to process or repair.
